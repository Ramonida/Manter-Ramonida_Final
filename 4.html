Programs Offered at Arellano University - CITE Department

The College of Information Technology Education (CITE) at Arellano University offers a variety of programs aimed at preparing students for careers in the fast-paced world of technology. Here are the programs offered:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) - This program equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge in software development, networking, database management, and other areas of IT.
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) - Students in this program delve deep into the theoretical foundations of computing, algorithms, programming languages, and software engineering.
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) - This program focuses on the design, implementation, and management of information systems in organizations, covering areas such as business processes, data management, and system analysis.
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) - Combining principles of computer science and electrical engineering, this program prepares students to design and develop computer hardware and software systems.
  • Associate in Computer Technology (ACT) - A two-year program that provides students with foundational knowledge in computer hardware, software, and networking, preparing them for entry-level positions in the IT industry or for further studies.

These programs are designed to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving technology industry, providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their chosen careers.
