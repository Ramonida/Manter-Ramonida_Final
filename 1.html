Arellano University is a private educational institution in the Philippines with campuses spread across Metro Manila. Established in 1938 by Florentino Cayco Sr., it began as a law school and eventually expanded its offerings to include programs in various fields such as education, engineering, business, arts, sciences, and healthcare. The university is known for its commitment to providing accessible and quality education to its students. It aims to mold individuals into competent professionals who are not only skilled in their respective fields but also imbued with values that promote social responsibility and leadership. Arellano University is particularly renowned for its law and nursing programs, which have produced numerous successful professionals in their respective fields. Over the years, it has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the Philippines, with a strong reputation for academic excellence and a dedication to student development.
