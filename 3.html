Arellano University CITE Department News

New Course Offering: Data Science Program
May 8, 2024

The Arellano University CITE Department is excited to announce the launch of its new Data Science program, aimed at preparing students for careers in the rapidly growing field of data analytics.

This comprehensive program covers topics such as data analysis, machine learning, data visualization, and big data technologies. Students will gain practical experience through hands-on projects and internships with industry partners.

CITE Department Receives Accreditation
April 22, 2024

We are proud to announce that the Arellano University CITE Department has been granted accreditation by the Accrediting Agency of Chartered Colleges and Universities in the Philippines (AACCUP).

This accreditation recognizes the department's commitment to academic excellence, quality instruction, and continuous improvement. It assures students and stakeholders of the high standards upheld by our programs.
